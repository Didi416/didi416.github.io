Hello, I'm Dyandra, an aspiring engineer and transdisciplinary practitioner, and here are a couple snapshots about who I am and projects I've worked on. I am currently studying a double degree Bachelor of Engineering (Honours) Bachelor of Creative Intelligence and Innovation, majoring in Mechatronics at UTS. Bachelor of Creaive Intelligence and Innovation (or BCII) is based in the Transdisciplinary School, where we believe in the importance of having multiple perspectives on a single subject. We learn to put aside our own biases and assumptions, and consult other disciplines and fields of study to gain a far more holistic approach to a world view and problem solving than we originally had in our monodisciplinary core degrees.
